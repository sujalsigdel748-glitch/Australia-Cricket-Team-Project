Australian Cricket Team

Welcome to my small website about the Australian cricket team. I made this using only HTML and tried to keep it very simple and beginner friendly. Here you can read about the team history, their big achievements and also see some players with their stats like runs, wickets, matches and short info. The English may be little messy but I hope its still easy to understand. You can click the Players page to explore the cricketers list with their images and roles.

What you will find here

This website includes small details like matches played, runs scored, wickets taken and best performances of players. It also shows trophies won by Australia and a short history of how cricket grew in the country. This site is made for my assignment so everything is in plain HTML only, no CSS or fancy stuff.
